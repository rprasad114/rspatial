This is a dataset with 397 cars.

mpg: Miles per gallon, the distance a car can drive with one gallon of gas.

cylinders: the number of cylinders.

displacement: engine displacement, i.e., the total volume of air that the pistons move in the engine's cylinders.

horsepower: engine's maximum power output

weight: The weight of the cars in lbs.

acceleration: the rate at which its velocity changes

year: model year

name: name of the car
